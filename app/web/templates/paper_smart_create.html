Strategy Mode Guide

Mode Behavior Best For Capital Profile
Conservative Heavier weighting to deeper zones, stronger average improvement if price drops more. Accumulation near deeper corrections. Highest expected total budget.
Balanced Middle weighting between early and deep zones using real support depth + score. General use (recommended). Medium expected total budget.
Aggressive More allocation in early zones to catch rebounds sooner. Fast rebounds / short swing entries. Lowest expected total budget.

Note: modes are not fixed percentages. Allocation is dynamically built from real support zones, support score, and depth context.
